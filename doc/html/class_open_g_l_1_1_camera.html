Detailed Description

Camera works as the human eye. All geometries inside its view bounds will be rendered. It offers 3 4x4 matrices useful to shading calculation, they are: model, view, projection. Those three matrices are also called MVP. In OpenGL the multiplication of this matrices is computed backwards, so we end up with PVM. Usually the camera matrices needed in shading are view and projection. The model one is used only to calculate cube maps or HDR.
